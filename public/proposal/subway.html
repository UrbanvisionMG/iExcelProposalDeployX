SEO Strategy Proposal for Subway.com

Executive Summary

Subway, as the world's largest submarine sandwich franchise, has tremendous potential to dominate local and national search results. Our comprehensive SEO analysis reveals significant opportunities to increase organic visibility, drive qualified traffic, and boost franchise location discovery across all markets. **Key Opportunity Areas:** - Local SEO optimization for 37,000+ locations - Menu and nutritional content optimization - Mobile-first user experience improvements - Franchise-specific landing page development

Current State Analysis

Strengths

  • Organic Traffic: 2.3M monthly visits with 65% mobile traffic
  • Keyword Rankings: Strong brand presence but missing local intent keywords
  • Technical Issues: Page speed optimization needed (3.2s average load time)
  • Local Presence: Inconsistent NAP data across franchise locations

Opportunities

  • • Local Search Dominance: Capture "sandwich near me" and location-based queries
  • • Menu Content Strategy: Optimize for ingredient and dietary preference searches
  • • Franchise Support: Improve individual location visibility and conversion
