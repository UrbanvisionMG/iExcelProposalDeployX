SEO Strategy Proposal for Subway.com

Executive Summary

Subway, as the world's largest submarine sandwich franchise, has tremendous potential to dominate local and national search results. Our comprehensive SEO analysis reveals significant opportunities to increase organic visibility, drive qualified traffic, and boost franchise location discovery across all markets.

Key Opportunity Areas:

  • Local SEO optimization for 37,000+ locations
  • Menu and nutritional content optimization
  • Mobile-first user experience improvements
  • Franchise-specific landing page development

Current SEO Performance Analysis

Findings

  • Organic Traffic: 2.3M monthly visits with 65% mobile traffic
  • Keyword Rankings: Strong brand presence but missing local intent keywords
  • Technical Issues: Page speed optimization needed (3.2s average load time)
  • Local Presence: Inconsistent NAP data across franchise locations

Opportunities

  • Local Search Dominance: Capture "sandwich near me" and location-based queries
  • Menu Content Strategy: Optimize for ingredient and dietary preference searches
  • Franchise Support: Improve individual location visibility and conversion

Recommended SEO Strategy

1. Local SEO Optimization

Timeline: 3-4 months

Investment: $8,500/month

  • Standardize NAP data across all 37,000+ locations
  • Optimize Google Business Profile for each franchise
  • Implement location-specific landing pages
  • Build local citation network and review management system

Expected Results:

  • 40% increase in "near me" search visibility
  • 25% improvement in local pack appearances
  • Enhanced franchise owner satisfaction and sales

2. Technical SEO Foundation

Timeline: 2-3 months

Investment: $6,200/month

  • Core Web Vitals optimization (target <2.5s load time)
  • Mobile-first indexing compliance
  • Schema markup implementation for menus and locations
  • Site architecture improvements for better crawlability

Expected Results:

  • 30% improvement in page speed scores
  • Better mobile user experience and rankings
  • Enhanced rich snippet appearances

3. Content Strategy & Optimization

Timeline: Ongoing

Investment: $4,800/month

  • Menu item optimization for ingredient-based searches
  • Nutritional content development for health-conscious queries
  • Franchise success stories and local community content
  • Seasonal promotion and limited-time offer optimization

Expected Results:

  • 50% increase in menu-related organic traffic
  • Improved engagement metrics and conversion rates
  • Stronger brand authority in the QSR space
