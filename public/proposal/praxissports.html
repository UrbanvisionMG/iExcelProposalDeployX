Executive Summary

Praxis Sports is a sports tech platform that simplifies scheduling, communication, and operations for clubs, academies, and teams - from youth to pro. The product is strong and the team is uniquely credible (Olympians, MLS veterans), but the **digital footprint is early-stage and fragmented**:

* Organic search is thin beyond branded terms; a handful of pages drive most traffic.
* Social channels (especially Instagram/TikTok) show promise, but content is not yet tied to a clear funnel or narrative.
* There is **no paid acquisition** currently running.
* Tracking, attribution, and GEO/SEO foundations can be strengthened to support scale.

At the same time, Praxis has clear strategic goals:

* Tell **one coherent story** about who you are, who you serve, and why you're different.
* Grow a **community of clubs and leaders** around that story.
* Turn that community into a **predictable pipeline** of demos, trials, and accounts - while respecting startup budgets.

iExcel will act as a **performance-focused growth partner**:

1. **Unify the narrative** across website, SEO, and social so Praxis feels like one clear, compelling brand.
2. **Build the digital foundation**: on-page SEO + GEO, conversion-focused web structure, and rock-solid tracking.
3. **Stand up first scalable acquisition engines**: bottom-of-funnel paid search, lightweight paid social, and targeted outbound (cold email + LinkedIn).
4. **Optimize for revenue and adoption**, not vanity metrics - weekly cycles of "what we did, what we learned, what we're doing next."
